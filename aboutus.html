About Us

Founded in 2025, One World Virtual is a premier alliance uniting several virtual airlines under a single banner within the Project Flight universe. Our mission is to create a truly global network of elite pilots and virtual aviation professionals.

Our Mission

We aim to deliver immersive experiences, promote advanced flight training, and provide an inclusive environment for pilots of all skill levels. Through innovative tools, events, and collaboration, we elevate the standards of virtual aviation.

What We Offer

Our members gain access to a wide network of airlines, dedicated support, structured ranking systems, and a rewards program tailored to recognize excellence and growth. We regularly host events, challenges, and partnerships across the community.

Meet the Team

Our leadership includes experienced virtual aviators, trainers, and developers, all driven by passion and commitment to quality. We work closely with Discord communities and tools like PFConnect and PF Wiki to deliver a seamless experience.

Join Us

Interested in being part of our alliance? Whether you're a solo pilot, airline owner, or simply curious, you're welcome to connect with us on Discord and begin your journey in the skies.

Forging the future of virtual aviation — one flight at a time.
